Multifunctional, modular and sustainable exhibition furniture for the outdoor clothing brand Ortovox.

Ortovox Sportartikel GmbH

Idea and concept development, Eco-design, Sustainable furniture, Mockup building, Visualizations, Project coordination with client; Collaborative project with Mara Regenfelder.

Designing for Sustainable Futures

How can exhibition furniture design enhance sustainability while showcasing products? What materials can be used for resource-efficient exhibition furniture? How can modular design improve exhibition furniture functionality? How does using upcycled materials affect sustainability perceptions?

Swiss wool

Swiss wool is vital to Ortovox for its superior thermal properties and climate control. Sourcing locally enhances sustainability while supporting regional economies.

www.ortovox.com/int-en/wool-expert/wool/swisswool

USP and Product range

The USP of Ortovox is focused on sustainability and locality, providing high-performance outdoor gear made from natural materials. Their product range includes technical apparel, insulation layers, and safety equipment.
